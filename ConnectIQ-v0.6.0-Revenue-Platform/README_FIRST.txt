CONNECTIQ v0.6.0 REVENUE PLATFORM

1. Extract this ZIP to a normal folder OUTSIDE C:\connectiqvscode.
2. Double-click install-connectiq.bat.
3. The installer creates a backup, preserves .env files, installs dependencies,
   runs lint, 100 tests, and the production build.
4. Use verify-install.bat to re-run the quality gate.
5. Use rollback.bat to restore the last installer backup.

Do not run the installer from inside the ZIP preview.
