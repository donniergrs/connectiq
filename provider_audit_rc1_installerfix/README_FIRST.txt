CONNECTIQ v0.4.0 - FIXED ONE-CLICK INSTALLER

1. Extract the ENTIRE ZIP to a temporary folder such as Downloads\ConnectIQ_v0.4.0_Fixed.
2. Do not run the installer from inside the ZIP.
3. Confirm package.json, functions, src, and install-connectiq-v0.4.0.bat are all visible together.
4. Double-click install-connectiq-v0.4.0.bat.
5. Press Enter to accept C:\connectiqvscode.

The installer preserves C:\connectiqvscode\.env and C:\connectiqvscode\functions\.env when they already exist.
